About the Student-Led Workshop System

What is this?

The Student-Led Workshop System is a platform designed to facilitate peer-led learning within our university community. It allows students to create, schedule, and participate in workshops led by fellow students.

Key Features

  • Workshop Creation: Hosts can create and manage workshops on various topics
  • Easy Registration: Students can browse and register for upcoming workshops
  • Attendance Tracking: Automated attendance recording and history
  • Feedback System: Collect and provide feedback to improve workshop quality
  • Automated Reminders: Email and SMS reminders for registered workshops
  • Role-Based Access: Different permissions for students, hosts, and administrators

User Roles

Students

Browse workshops, register to attend, track participation, and provide feedback.

Hosts

Create workshops, manage registrations, mark attendance, and view feedback.

Admins

Approve host requests, manage users, oversee all workshops, and generate reports.

How It Works

  1. Register: Create an account with your student number
  2. Choose Your Path: Register as a student or apply to be a host
  3. Get Involved:
    • As a student: Browse and join workshops that interest you
    • As a host: Create workshops and share your knowledge
  4. Participate: Attend workshops, learn, and grow together
  5. Provide Feedback: Help improve the community by sharing your thoughts

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All personal information is stored securely and used only for workshop management purposes. Passwords are encrypted, and access is controlled through role-based permissions.
