Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026 | Compliant with POPIA Act 4 of 2013

📋 Your Privacy Rights: This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Responsible Party

The Student-Led Workshop System is the responsible party for the processing of your personal information.
Contact: privacy@workshop-system.ac.za

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum necessary information to provide our services:

  • Student Number - Required for verification of student status
  • Email Address - For communication and account verification
  • Full Name - For identification and workshop certificates
  • Password (encrypted) - For secure authentication
  • Role - Student, Host, or Administrator
  • Login timestamps - For security and usage analytics

3. Why We Process Your Information

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • To provide access to workshop creation and registration features
  • To manage workshop attendance and feedback
  • To send important service notifications (required)
  • To comply with legal obligations (POPIA, institutional policies)
  • To improve our services through anonymized analytics

4. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA Section 11)

We process your information based on:

  • Consent - When you register, you consent to necessary processing
  • Contract Performance - To provide requested workshop services
  • Legal Obligation - To comply with applicable laws
  • Legitimate Interest - For system security and improvement

5. Your Data Rights (POPIA Chapter 3)

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access - View all data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction - Update inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion - Request account deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Object - Opt-out of optional communications
  • Right to Data Portability - Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Restrict Processing - Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent - Change your notification preferences

Exercise Your Rights

6. How We Protect Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption at Rest - Passwords hashed using bcrypt
  • Encryption in Transit - HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
  • Access Controls - Role-based permissions and authentication
  • Audit Logging - All data access logged for accountability
  • Regular Security Reviews - Ongoing vulnerability assessments

7. Data Retention Period

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts - Retained while you use the service
  • Inactive Accounts - Marked inactive after 2 years of no login
  • Deleted Accounts - Permanently removed 30 days after deletion request
  • Workshop Records - Archived after 5 years for institutional memory

8. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share limited data with:

  • Email Services - For sending notifications (with your consent)
  • SMS Services - For urgent reminders (opt-in only)
  • Hosting Provider - Secure cloud infrastructure (POPIA-compliant)
  • Institutional Systems - As required by university policies

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data per POPIA standards.

9. Cookies Policy

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies - Session management (required for login)
  • Preference Cookies - Remember your settings
  • Analytics Cookies - Understand usage patterns (anonymous)

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

10. Automated Decision Making

We do NOT use automated decision-making or profiling that significantly affects you.

11. Cross-Border Data Transfers

If data is transferred outside South Africa, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy determinations
  • Explicit user consent

12. Data Breach Response

In case of a data breach:

  1. We will assess the risk within 72 hours
  2. Notify the Information Regulator if high risk
  3. Notify affected users if necessary
  4. Take immediate remedial action

Report suspected breaches to: security@workshop-system.ac.za

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email and require renewed consent.

14. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions:

  • Email: privacy@workshop-system.ac.za
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@workshop-system.ac.za
  • Information Regulator (SA): www.justice.gov.za/inforeg
